# ⚖️ LawBridge

## AI-Powered Multilingual Legal Awareness Platform

LawBridge is an AI-powered web application designed to bridge the gap between Sri Lankan citizens and legal information.

The platform helps users understand legal issues, explain legal documents, and identify the correct first step through simple explanations in Sinhala, Tamil, and English.

LawBridge combines Artificial Intelligence, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and OCR technology to provide accessible legal awareness while ensuring users understand that it does not replace professional legal advice.

---

## 🌍 Problem

Many Sri Lankan citizens experience legal problems but struggle to understand their rights and available actions because:

- Legal language is complex
- Professional legal consultation can be expensive
- Basic legal information is difficult to access
- Multilingual legal resources are limited

As a result, people may delay action or make incorrect decisions regarding employment issues, rental disputes, consumer complaints, and legal documents.

---

## 💡 Solution

LawBridge provides an accessible digital platform where citizens can:

- Ask legal questions using natural language
- Receive simple legal explanations
- Understand uploaded legal documents
- Explore common legal topics
- Save useful legal guidance

Unlike general legal chatbots, LawBridge focuses on Sri Lankan citizens by providing multilingual support and retrieving relevant local legal information.

# ✨ Key Features

## 🤖 AI Legal Guidance Assistant

- Goal-Based Legal Guidance Agent
- Legal issue classification
- RAG-based legal information retrieval
- Simple language explanations
- Practical first-step recommendations


## 🌐 Multilingual Support

Users can interact in:

- Sinhala
- Tamil
- English


## 📄 AI Document Explanation

Users can upload:

- Notices
- Agreements
- Contracts
- Legal letters

The system uses OCR to extract text and AI to explain the document in simple language.


## 📚 Legal Knowledge Hub

Users can browse information about:

- Labour Law
- Tenancy Issues
- Consumer Protection


## 👤 User Management

- Registration and login
- Profile management
- Chat history
- Saved answers


## 🔐 Admin Management

Admins can:

- Manage legal knowledge content
- Update legal categories
- Review uploaded legal information
- Monitor system usage
